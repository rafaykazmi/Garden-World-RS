How to Use Sunflower Seeds

Grow beautiful sunflowers in 4 simple steps

Step-by-Step Guide

Prepare Soil

Choose sunny spot, loosen soil 1-2 feet deep, mix with compost

Sow Seeds

Plant 1-2 inches deep, 6-12 inches apart in spring after frost

Water & Support

Keep soil moist, support tall stems with stakes as they grow

Harvest Seeds

When heads droop & petals fall, cut heads to dry indoors
